About us

We are Right hand charity

Making the world a better place

Right hand charity is the largest technology platform for crowdfunding in the healthcare sector . We offer a complete range of free healthcare services including medical and surgical treatments, medical camps and tele-health facility to the underprivileged community of world. Our goal is to reach the millions of needy patients all over world who are suffering because of lack of healthcare facilities. We continue to serve the humanity at large by providing the best healthcare in the world
